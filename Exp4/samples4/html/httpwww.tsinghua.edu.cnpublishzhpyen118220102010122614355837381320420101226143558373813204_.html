2010-7-12 Nonlinear variational wave equations

报告题目: Nonlinear variational wave equations 
报告人: Yuxi Zheng (郑玉玺) 


摘要: We present some basic results of existence of solutions to differential equations that includes Einstein’s field equations of general relativity, nematic liquid crystals, and shallow water equations.


报告人简介：Professor Yuxi Zheng received his PhD from UC Berkeley in 1990, got Sloan fellowship in 1995, and has published three books. His main research field is partial differential equations and is in the editorial broads of a number of top mathematical journals.


报告时间: 7月12日 16：00-17：00 pm（周一）
报告地点: 清华大学科学馆 104 会议室
