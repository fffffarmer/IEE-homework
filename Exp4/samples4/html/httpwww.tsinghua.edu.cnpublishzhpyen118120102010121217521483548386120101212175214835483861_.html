Applied Analysis

Modeling irreversible processes via (hyperbolic) partial differential equations, mathematically analyzing and numerically solving such equations, with applications to multi-phase flows, neurosciences, radiation hydrodynamics, chemically reactive flows, Maxwell fluid flows, lattice Boltzmann method, and so on.
