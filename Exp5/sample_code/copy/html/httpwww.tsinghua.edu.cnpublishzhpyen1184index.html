Postdoc

       Zhou Pei-Yuan Center for Applied Mathematics is an institute for carrying out interdisciplinary basic sciences research. The ‘Center’ is devoted to: pushing forward the interdisciplinary research and the cooperation between mathematics and empirical sciences; promoting the development of applied mathematics and empirical sciences at Tsinghua University; developing international academic exchanges and  cooperation; fostering excellent talents with innovative abilities. The ‘Center’ is honored to have Professor C.C. Lin (a renowned applied mathematician, a member of the American National Academy of Sciences and a Foreign Member of the Chinese Academy of Sciences) as its Honorary Director, who has provided guidance and support to the Center’s development since its inception.

       We are currently engaged in: theoretical biology (protein folding, systems biology, computational neuroscience and etc.) and methods of applied mathematics (Applied Partial Differential Equation, scientific computation, probability of statistics, nonlinear waves theory and etc.)

       Our researchers include professors, researchers, associate researchers and post-docs. The ‘Center’ invites applications for post-doctoral fellows. People with doctor’s degree under the age of 35 can apply for post-doctoral fellows. Post-doctoral fellows should go through public evaluation and obtain permission from National Administrative Committee of Post-Doctoral. Period of work: 2 years.

       Application materials (including application form of Tsinghua University Post-doctorate, resume, area of research, list of published paper, copies of some important paper, recommendation letters from two experts of the relevant area).
